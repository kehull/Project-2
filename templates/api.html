Finding Safe Harbor in California

California is one of the biggest and most populous states in the entire US. But just how safe is it to live and work there?

By looking at the number and size of earthquakes and wildfires- two of California's most common national disasters- as well as the counties in which these disasters take place and the frequency with which those counties experience wildfires and earthquakes, we can determine which counties are the safest and most dangerous.
